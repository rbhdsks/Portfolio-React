**Deep Learning · Generative Adversarial Networks · Sept 2023 – May 2024 · Ladakh, India**

A vision-guided robot designed to follow a person reliably even in heavy rain and at night — conditions where standard perception pipelines fail.

- Built **GAN-based data augmentation** and feature-preservation pipelines so the robot can "see" in rainy, low-light conditions.
- Implemented a **synthetic rain generator (RainGAN)** and **CycleGAN** for cross-domain image translation.
- Achieved an **FID score of 31.4** on generated scenes.
- Selected and funded under **NewGenIEDC-IIITA** and chosen by the **Indian Army**.

Related publications: *RainGAN-Kathmandu* (IEEE TENCON 2025) and *ADAS Framework Utilizing CycleGAN* (CONFLUENCE 2025).
