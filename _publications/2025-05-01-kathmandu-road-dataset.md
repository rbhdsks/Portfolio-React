An open urban road-scene dataset capturing the Ratnapark-to-Tripureshwor corridor in Kathmandu, intended to support research on perception in dense South Asian traffic and adverse weather. Available on **Harvard Dataverse**: [doi.org/10.7910/DVN/38E8AG](https://doi.org/10.7910/DVN/38E8AG).
